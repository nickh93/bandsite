about

The xx are an English alternative band formed in 2005 in Wandsworth, London.[3] The group released its debut album, xx, in August 2009. The album was a commercial and critical success and was ranked highly on many best of 2009 lists, including number one on the list compiled by The Guardian and second for NME.[4][5] In 2010, the band won the Mercury Music Prize for its debut album. Their second album, Coexist, was released on 10 September 2012
